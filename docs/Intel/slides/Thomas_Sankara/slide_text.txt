* "Africa's Che Guevara" — shot dead in a coup at age 37. His killer was CONVICTED in 2022.
* In FOUR YEARS he vaccinated 2.5 million children, planted 10 million trees, outlawed forced marriage, and made Burkina Faso food self-sufficient.
* Three months before his death, he warned: whoever REFUSES to repay foreign debt ALONE will be killed. He was the only one who refused.
* His death certificate listed "NATURAL CAUSES" — for a man shot more than a DOZEN times.
* The commander of his assassination was later awarded France's LEGION OF HONOR.
