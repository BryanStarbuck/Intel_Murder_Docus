* 31-year-old American journalist EXECUTED by the Chilean military during the 1973 coup. His own government helped get him killed.
* A Chilean court ruled US military intelligence played a "FUNDAMENTAL ROLE" in his murder.
* He was targeted because he WITNESSED American military involvement in the coup and took DETAILED notes.
* His father flew to Chile searching for him — OBSTRUCTED by both Chilean authorities and the US Embassy.
* Harvard graduate. His story became the 1982 film MISSING starring Jack Lemmon. His wife fought for justice for DECADES.
