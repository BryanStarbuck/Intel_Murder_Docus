STEVEN HOFFENBERG - DETAILED FINDINGS

Full Name: Steven Jude Hoffenberg
Born: January 12, 1945
Died: August 2022 (body found August 23, 2022)
Age at Death: 77
Location of Death: Derby, Connecticut, USA
Cause of Death: Pending toxicology results
Official Ruling: No signs of trauma, no struggle, no forced entry

DISCOVERY OF BODY:
- Body found Tuesday, August 23, 2022
- Believed to have died at least 7 days before discovery
- Body in advanced state of decomposition
- Had to be identified through dental records
- Police responded to welfare check request
- Request came from private investigator working for an Epstein sexual abuse victim
- Woman said she hadn't heard from Hoffenberg for 5 days, which was unusual
- Living in small apartment in multifamily home in Derby, CT (12 miles from Bridgeport)

AUTOPSY FINDINGS:
- No signs of trauma
- No indications of struggle at apartment
- No forced entry
- Cause of death pending toxicology test results
- Medical examiners confirmed identity Friday, August 26, 2022

RELATIONSHIP WITH EPSTEIN:
- Described as Epstein's "mentor"
- Epstein worked for Hoffenberg's company Towers Financial Corp in late 1980s
- Had a "special relationship" according to lawyer Gary Baise
- Hoffenberg said Epstein was "the smartest person he knew when it came to money"
- Claimed Epstein was actually the architect of the Ponzi scheme
- Epstein was never charged in the Towers Financial fraud

TOWERS FINANCIAL PONZI SCHEME:
- Founded, CEO, president, and chairman of Towers Financial Corporation
- Bill collection company
- Ran Ponzi scheme between 1988 and 1993
- One of the country's largest frauds at the time
- Swindled thousands of investors out of $460 million (some sources say $475 million)
- Pleaded guilty in 1995
- Sentenced in 1997 to 20 years in federal prison
- Actually served 18 years
- Released from federal custody in 2013

NEW YORK POST CONNECTION:
- Once tried buying the New York Post
- Briefly took over the Post in 1993 while bidding to own it
- Funded the paper for three months
- Rescued it from bankruptcy
- Efforts to buy paper derailed by SEC civil fraud allegations
- Led to criminal prosecution of Ponzi case

CLAIMS ABOUT EPSTEIN AND MOSSAD:
- Confessed to National Enquirer that Epstein and Maxwell were taping honey-trap videos
- Said videos were of VIPs with underage girls for blackmail cabal
- Cooperated with FBI about Epstein
- Spoke about Epstein's alleged Mossad connections
- Former Israeli intelligence officer Ari Ben-Menashe claims Epstein and Maxwell ran Mossad "honeytrap"

PERSONAL CHARACTERISTICS:
