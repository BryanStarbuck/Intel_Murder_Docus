PALM BEACH HOTEL DEATHS:

1. Carolyn Andriano (2023)
   - Age: 36
   - Date of Death: May 23, 2023
   - Location: DoubleTree by Hilton, West Palm Beach, Florida
   - Cause: Accidental overdose involving methadone, fentanyl, and alprazolam
   - Background:
     * Recruited to Jeffrey Epstein's mansion at age 14
     * Testified against Ghislaine Maxwell in 2021
     * Long history of addiction tied to trauma from abuse
     * Death not announced publicly until months later
     * Mother questioned the circumstances
     * Police and toxicology reports classified death as accidental
     * Left behind five children

2. Leigh Skye Patrick (2017)
   - Age: 29
   - Date of Death: 2017
   - Location: Hotel in West Palm Beach, Florida
   - Cause: Fatal overdose (details not fully released publicly)
   - Background:
     * Twin sister stated Leigh's addiction and struggles began after encounters with Epstein
     * First known Epstein survivor in Palm Beach County to die of overdose

CONTEXT:
- Both women were confirmed victims of Epstein's abuse
- Both deaths occurred in hotel rooms in West Palm Beach, years apart
- Trauma-linked addiction widely documented among survivors of sexual exploitation
- Overdose risk significantly elevated in such populations
- Official investigations have not identified pattern of homicides or coordinated effort to silence witnesses
