ALFREDO RODRIGUEZ - DETAILED FINDINGS

Full Name: Alfredo Rodriguez
Age at Death: 60
Date of Death: December 28, 2014
Cause of Death: Mesothelioma (rare cancer)
Official Ruling: Natural death

ROLE WITH EPSTEIN:
- Butler/house manager to Jeffrey Epstein
- Worked at Epstein's Palm Beach mansion
- Had access to Epstein's private records and documentation

THE BLACK BOOK:
- Rodriguez stole Epstein's "black book" - a 97-page journal
- Book allegedly "detailed the full scope and the extent of Epstein's involvement with underage girls"
- Contained contact details of Epstein's celebrity friends and associates
- Included names like Bill Clinton, Donald Trump, Prince Andrew, and many others
- Over 1,500 names, phone numbers, and addresses
- Prosecutors called it "The Holy Grail" in 2011
- Rodriguez claimed he needed it as insurance to protect his own life

LEGAL TROUBLES:
- 2009: Rodriguez arrested for withholding evidence
- Failed to tell prosecutors he possessed the book
- Refused to hand it over to authorities
- Attempted to sell the book for $50,000 (£31,000)
- 2010: Sentenced to 18 months in federal prison for obstruction of justice
- Sentence was same length as Epstein's own sentence (ironic)
- Served his sentence before dying in 2014

WIDOW'S CLAIMS:
- Widow: Patricia Dunn
- Dunn told DailyMail.com that Rodriguez "knew all about Prince Andrew"
- This was in context of Prince Andrew being named in Epstein sex scandal
- Allegations that Prince Andrew was supplied with teenage girl by Epstein

LOCATION OF BLACK BOOK:
- Current location of the document has not been disclosed
- According to The Mirror, Epstein's lawyers were "petrified" about where it would go after Rodriguez's death
- Book first brought to light in 2009 when Rodriguez was arrested

CONSPIRACY THEORIES:
- Some question whether mesothelioma death was natural given his possession of damaging evidence
- Timing of death (3 years after serving sentence) raised suspicions among conspiracy theorists
- However, widow confirmed natural death from mesothelioma
- Mesothelioma is typically caused by asbestos exposure and has long latency period

SIGNIFICANCE:
- Rodriguez was one of few people with direct documentary evidence of Epstein's activities
- His black book became crucial evidence in understanding scope of Epstein's network
- Death meant loss of potential witness with insider knowledge
- Book's contents have been partially leaked and published online over years
