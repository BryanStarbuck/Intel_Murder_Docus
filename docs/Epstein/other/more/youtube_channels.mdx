## Channel Deletions — A Pattern Worth Noting

A recurring pattern across this research: many of the most dedicated Epstein and energy suppression investigators have had their YouTube channels deleted or severely restricted:

| Creator | What Happened |
|---------|---------------|
| **James Corbett** (Corbett Report) | Fully removed from YouTube |
| **Ryan Dawson** | Fully removed — 80K subscribers, 15 years of content lost |
| **Jake Morphonios** (Blackstone Intelligence) | Fully removed — 134K+ subscribers, detailed Epstein investigation |
| **Mouthy Buddha** | Fully removed — created viral deep-dive videos on elite crime networks |
| **Amazing Polly** | Removed — early researcher on Epstein + science/eugenics connections |
| **Ben Swann** | Removed — one of earliest mainstream journalists on Epstein |
| **Shaun Attwood** | Had 40% of content (60M views) deleted; stopped Epstein coverage temporarily |

These creators have migrated to **Rumble**, **BitChute**, **Odyssey**, **Substack**, and their own websites. The pattern of deletion itself is documented as part of the broader suppression pattern this project tracks.
