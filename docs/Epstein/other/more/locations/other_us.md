## Monte Rio, California (Sonoma County)

- **[Bohemian Grove](../groups/bohemian_grove.md)** — Secretive 2,700-acre all-male elite retreat where presidents, CIA directors, billionaires, and defense contractors network privately each July. The Santa Rosa Hitchhiker Murders (1972–1979) — seven unsolved murders of young women in nearby Sonoma County — have no established connection to the Grove but provide geographic context.
