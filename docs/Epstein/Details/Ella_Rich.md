## Assessment: SUSPICIOUS

Rich was killed alongside her fiance [Frank Yassenoff](Frank_Yassenoff.md), a business partner of [Berry Kessler](Berry_Kessler.md). Kessler was suspected but never charged. The double murder is part of a pattern of deaths around Kessler and the Columbus financial network connected to Les Wexner, which later enabled Epstein's rise to power.

## Circumstances of Death

Ella Rich was found shot to death alongside her fiance Frank Yassenoff in Columbus, Ohio on March 23, 1970. The case remains unsolved.

## Connection to the Epstein Network

Rich was connected to the Epstein network through her fiance Yassenoff and his business partner [Berry Kessler](Berry_Kessler.md). Kessler was the prime suspect in the 1985 murder of [Arthur Shapiro](Arthur_Shapiro.md) — the killing that created the opening for Epstein to take over Les Wexner's finances — and was later convicted of the 1991 murder-for-hire of [John Deroo](John_Deroo.md).
