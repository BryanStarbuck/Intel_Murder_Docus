

# Monica Petersen

University of Denver trafficking researcher who died in Haiti at age 32; her work focused on labor exploitation in Haitian industrial parks.

## Key Facts

- **Full Name:** Monica Petersen
- **Born:** ~1984
- **Died:** November 13, 2016, Haiti (age ~32)
- **Cause of Death:** Ruled suicide (details not publicly disclosed)
- **Official Ruling:** Self-inflicted death

## Connection to Epstein

Monica Petersen's connection to the Epstein network is tangential, running through trafficking research and Clinton Foundation activities in Haiti:

- Petersen was a Research Fellow and Assistant Director at the Human Trafficking Center at the University of Denver's Korbel School of International Studies
- She had traveled to Haiti multiple times and was working on the ground there when she died
- Haiti is a documented nexus for trafficking — and Epstein associate [Jean-Luc Brunel](Jean_Luc_Brunel.md) operated modeling recruitment in the Caribbean
- Bill Clinton's extensive involvement in Haiti through the Clinton Foundation, combined with his documented relationship with Epstein, created a speculative connection
- The Clintons' Haiti involvement includes the Caracol Industrial Park — Petersen had researched labor exploitation in such facilities

## Circumstances of Death

On November 13, 2016:

- Petersen was found dead in Haiti
- The Human Trafficking Center's director, Claude d'Estrée, told the Washington Post that the death was a suicide
- D'Estrée stated Petersen was not in Haiti to research human trafficking or investigate the Clintons — she had been teaching and exploring setting up an NGO
- No detailed public account of the circumstances has been released
- Her death coincided with the height of the 2016 "Pizzagate" conspiracy theories

## Controversy

- Conspiracy theorists claimed Petersen was investigating Clinton Foundation trafficking in Haiti and was killed for it
- Fact-checkers (Snopes, PolitiFact, Washington Post) have noted there is no evidence linking the Clintons to her death or to trafficking in Haiti
- Her colleagues at the University of Denver described her death as a personal tragedy unrelated to any investigation
- The lack of public detail about the circumstances has fueled speculation

## Assessment

The connection to Epstein is speculative, running through Haiti and the Clinton overlap. Mainstream fact-checkers have debunked the specific claim that she was killed for investigating Clinton trafficking. However, her expertise in trafficking, her presence in Haiti, and the lack of public details about her death keep her name circulating in these discussions.

**Suspicion Level:** UNCERTAIN — Trafficking researcher died in Haiti. No direct Epstein link. Conspiracy claims debunked by mainstream sources, but limited public details.
