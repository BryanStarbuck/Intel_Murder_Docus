## Background

Terje Rød-Larsen is a Norwegian diplomat and politician who played a key role in the Oslo Accords (1993) that attempted Israeli-Palestinian peace. He held senior positions at the United Nations and in Norwegian government. His wife is [Mona Juul](Mona_Juul.mdx), who served as Norwegian ambassador.
