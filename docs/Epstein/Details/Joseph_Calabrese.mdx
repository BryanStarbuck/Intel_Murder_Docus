## Assessment: SUSPICIOUS

Calabrese was a Brooklyn Homicide detective found dead just one day after Deputy Chief Steven Silks. Two senior NYPD members dying on consecutive days was described by officials as unprecedented. As a homicide detective, he would have had investigative skills and access relevant to examining evidence on seized devices. His death is the second in the June 2019 cluster.

## Circumstances of Death

Detective Joseph Calabrese, 58, was found dead at Plum Beach in Brooklyn on June 6, 2019 — one day after Deputy Chief [Steven Silks](Steven_Silks.md) was found dead in Queens. He worked in Brooklyn Homicide.

## Connection to Weiner Laptop

Calabrese is named in online claims as one of 12 NYPD officers who allegedly viewed files on Anthony Weiner's laptop. As a homicide detective, he is alleged to have had relevant investigative access. These claims have not been independently verified by credible news sources. The FBI, not the NYPD, had formal custody of the laptop.
