

# John Ashe

Former UN General Assembly president who died when a barbell crushed his throat at home, days before his corruption trial involving a major Clinton Foundation donor.

## Key Facts

- **Full Name:** John William Ashe
- **Born:** 1954, Antigua and Barbuda
- **Died:** June 22, 2016, Dobbs Ferry, New York (age 61)
- **Cause of Death:** Traumatic asphyxia and laryngeal fractures from dropped barbell
- **Official Ruling:** Accident

## Connection to Epstein

John Ashe's connection to Epstein is indirect but runs through overlapping power networks:

- Ashe was charged with accepting $1.3 million in bribes from Chinese billionaire Ng Lap Seng, a major Clinton Foundation donor
- The Clinton Foundation's financial networks overlap with Epstein's — Bill Clinton flew on Epstein's jet 26+ times and visited his island
- The manner of death — crushing injury to the throat/neck while alone — drew comparisons to Epstein's own injuries during his first reported "suicide attempt" in jail
- The UN initially misreported his cause of death as a "heart attack" before the medical examiner corrected it to traumatic asphyxia
- Ashe was set to appear in court days after his death

## Circumstances of Death

On June 22, 2016, Ashe was found dead in his home in Dobbs Ferry, New York:

- He was apparently lifting a barbell on a bench press when the weight dropped onto his neck
- The Westchester County medical examiner determined the cause was traumatic asphyxia with laryngeal cartilage fractures
- He was alone at the time of death
- The UN's initial statement said he died of a heart attack — this was later corrected
- He was awaiting trial on federal corruption charges and was expected to appear for pre-trial proceedings in the coming days

## Controversy

- The timing — days before his court appearance — raised suspicion
- The unusual manner of death (a barbell crushing the throat) is extremely rare
- The UN's initial misstatement about the cause of death fueled further speculation
- Fact-checkers note that Ashe was not scheduled to "testify against Hillary Clinton" as some claimed; his case concerned UN bribery, not Clinton Foundation activities directly
- However, the bribe-payer Ng Lap Seng had previously been involved in illegal donations to Democrats in the 1990s "Chinagate" scandal

## Assessment

The death was ruled accidental by the medical examiner. The connection to Epstein is tangential, running through Clinton Foundation donor networks rather than direct Epstein involvement. However, the extremely unusual cause of death and the timing before his trial make this case notable.
