Deroo's murder is directly connected to the Epstein network through [Berry Kessler](Berry_Kessler.md), the accountant who was also the prime suspect in the 1985 murder of [Arthur Shapiro](Arthur_Shapiro.md) — the man whose death created the opening for Epstein to take over Les Wexner's finances. ## Circumstances of Death

John Deroo was shot six times in the face at his Custom Craft Cabinetry shop in Hudson, Florida on February 3, 1991. The killing was a contract hit arranged by his business partner Berry Kessler.

## The Kessler Pattern

Deroo's murder is part of a pattern of deaths surrounding Berry Kessler and the Columbus, Ohio financial network connected to Les Wexner:

- **1970:** [Frank Yassenoff](Frank_Yassenoff.md) and [Ella Rich](Ella_Rich.md) shot to death — Kessler suspected, never charged
- **~1970s:** [Marjorie Dyer](Marjorie_Dyer.md) died in questionable car accident — co-signed Yassenoff's will with Kessler
- **1985:** [Arthur Shapiro](Arthur_Shapiro.md) gunned down — Kessler was prime suspect; Epstein took Shapiro's position
- **1991:** John Deroo shot six times — Kessler convicted of murder-for-hire

Kessler's documented willingness to arrange contract killings adds weight to suspicions about the unsolved Shapiro murder, which directly enabled Epstein's rise to power.
