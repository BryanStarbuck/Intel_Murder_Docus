## Circumstances of Death

Sergeant Terrence McAvoy died on July 27, 2019. Limited details about the specific circumstances of his death are available in public records compared to the earlier June deaths, which received extensive national media coverage.

## Connection to Weiner Laptop

McAvoy is named in online claims as one of 12 NYPD officers who allegedly viewed files on Anthony Weiner's laptop. These claims have not been independently verified. The FBI, not the NYPD, had formal custody of the laptop.
